I'm a bioinformatician at the [Simons Initiative for the Developing Brain](https://www.sidb.org.uk/) and the Edinburgh Centre of the [Dementia Research Institute](https://ukdri.ac.uk/centres/edinburgh). My [team](https://www.sidb.org.uk/owen-dando/) focuses on the application of high-throughput sequencing and related technologies to study neurodevelopmental and neurodegenerative disorders.
